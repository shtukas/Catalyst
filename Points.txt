
Point, are the center of the Catalyst universe, the abstract data carriers. They are elements of Cliques and mutate using Fragments.

{
    "uuid"             : String
    "nyxNxSet"         : "6b240037-8f5f-4f52-841d-12106658171f"
    "unixtime"         : Float # Unixtime with decimals
}

They are targetted by DecriptionsZs, DateTimeZs, Tags, Notes and Comments.
